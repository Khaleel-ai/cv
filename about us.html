Tamyeez Publications

We are a Salafi publishing company set put to connect the Muslims to the Scholars of Ahlus Sunnah wal Jama'aah. We will have translations of Books; some of which will be in E-Book format, and some will be for sale. There will also be audios; some of which will be from sermons, others from lectures, and others from classes, and the classes will be the more so of the audios. That is because, the classes is how the knowledge is obtained.

May Allah aid us in this affair and bless the Muslims in their knowledge, their wealth, their health, and their families, Ameen.
